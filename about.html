About Me

I'm a software engineer who works remotely for a startup company. I'm curently living in Mexico City to experience a new culture as well as learn how to speak Spanish--this is still a work in progress! After reading about the collaborative philosophy of Fab Academy and looking over some projects from years past, I was inspired to try it myself. Coming from software, I've always loved the notion that you can dream up an idea and be able to bring it to fruition (with certain constraints, of course...and bugs, there are always a lot of bugs). Doing this in the physical realm seems like an even more empowering experience!
